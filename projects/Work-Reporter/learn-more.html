Work Reporter

Work Reporter is a Simple and Minimal Report Recording app.

Work Reporter can be used:

  • To keep track of Daily Services
  • To record daily progress of your projects
  • And many more that you can think of as your need

Features:

  • Work Reporter currently supports 3 Tracking Points / Projects
  • Once the initial Setup is Complete you can use Work Reporter to keep tally of your daily with respect to the given Tracking Points
