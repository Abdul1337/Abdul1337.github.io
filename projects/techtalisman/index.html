Our Products Empower Schools to get digital, improve processes and learning
There are multiple utilities for a coaching institute. Our mission is to make each one of them possible through one single platform. Take a quick look at how we help institutes of all sizes grow bigger, grow faster!
LMS (Learning Management System):

Just like Word helps you write documents and Gmail helps you manage your emails, LMS helps you to create, manage, and deliver eLearning courses. Now, create lessons, organize them into courses, deliver the content, enroll students to desired courses, and finally, monitor and assess their performance from anywhere and anytime.

ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning):

Think of all the core services required to run a school: accounts, queries, admissions, fees, and a lot more. ERP helps you integrate all these processes into a single system and makes your management hassle-free. Now get higher productivity and better insights for your school for a better tomorrow.

Assessment:

Want your students to practice more and make them brush up the concepts regularly? Now upload assignments online and make teaching more efficient than ever before. Students can upload answers to the assignments and can also raise doubts if any. Help your students to gain access to the maximum number of questions and help them become confident about the lessons they learn.

IoT (Internet of Things):

With IoT bringing in a revolution in the present-day world, we have already integrated it into our systems to make our products even more efficient and also make things easier for you. With Alexa powered ERP and biometric integration, we surely are bringing a revolution in the education system. Along with a tablet and a portable printer used for managing visitors.
